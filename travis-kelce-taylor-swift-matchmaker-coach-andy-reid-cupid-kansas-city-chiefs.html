Travis Kelce jokes coach Andy Reid may have been Taylor Swift matchmaker - 'who knew cupid was so bi

Andy Reid was connecting with Taylor Swift before Travis Kelce.

The Kansas City Chiefs head coach joked last Sunday that he helped 'set up' Kelce and Swift.

Kelce, appearing on the recent New Heights podcast with his brother, Jason, revealed an even deeper connection between the famous Chiefs leader and an even more famous pop music superstar.

“I heard he (Reid) had met the Swift family before so he could have been in the background the whole time,” Travis said. “When he was a Philly coach, yeah, that's when he met the Swift family. Who knows, he could have been in the background the whole time. Shout out to coach Reid."

After Jason joked that Reid had played “matchmaker,” Travis increased the brotherly humor.

"Who knew cupid was so big!" Travis said.
