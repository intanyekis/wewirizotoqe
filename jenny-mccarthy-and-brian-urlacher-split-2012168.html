Jenny McCarthy and Brian Urlacher Split!

Jenny McCarthy is singled out yet again.

After being linked to Chicago Bears linebacker Brian Urlacher in April, the 39-year-old model and TV host tells Us Weekly they have "decided to turn our romance into an amazing friendship. I will continue to be the biggest cheerleader for him. Go, Bears!"

During a July 16 appearance on Chelsea Lately, McCarthy opened up about her relationship with the NFL player while chatting with longtime pal Chelsea Handler, 37. "It's going really good. I didn't have to find love in the wild," McCarthy joked, referring her NBC dating series. "I found it on the football field."

"That's very exciting. I'm happy for you," Handler told McCarthy. "You were dating some real f-cking losers."
