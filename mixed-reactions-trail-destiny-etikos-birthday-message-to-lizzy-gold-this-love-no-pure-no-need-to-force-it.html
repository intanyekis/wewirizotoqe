Mixed reactions trail Destiny Etikos birthday message to Lizzy Gold: This love no pure, no



Destiny Etiko, a Nollywood actress, has sparked a discussion with her birthday message to her colleague and friend, Lizzy Gold, who is celebrating her birthday today.

On her Instagram page, Destiny shared a photo of Lizzy Gold and sent her birthday wishes.

She prayed for Lizzy to have a long life filled with prosperity and abundant grace.

“Happy birthday to you GHEN GHEN @lizzygoldofficial
Wishing you long life and prosperity with heavenly blessings
More undiluted grace”
Cheers.”

Lizzy Gold thanked Destiny for the birthday wishes in the comment section.

“Thanks, Drama Doll. I appreciate it,” she wrote.

However, not everyone was impressed with Destiny’s simple birthday message. Some criticized her for trying too hard to maintain her friendship with Lizzy. It was pointed out that Destiny was only reciprocating the love Lizzy showed her on her own birthday in August.

One comment from the Official House of Bella read, “This love no pure.”

Another comment from Youngress Ye said, “Fake! Fake! There’s no need to force it. The friendship is over but I understand she posted u on ur birthday too.”

It can be recalled that in February 2022, Lizzy Gold spoke up about a fight she had with Destiny Etiko on a movie set.

A video shared on Instagram captured Destiny Etiko questioning Lizzy Gold about her visit to an unknown location without permission.

Lizzy appeared to be confused by the question and ignored Destiny, who then pushed her, causing a scene that required the crew members to intervene.

Destiny Etiko accused Lizzy Gold of being ungrateful and referred to her as a chameleon and a devil.

In response, Lizzy Gold declared on her Instagram page that she was done making friends, citing the issue with Destiny Etiko.

However, the two have since reconciled, as Destiny surprised Lizzy in a grand manner on her birthday and expressed kind words towards her.

Earlier this year, Lizzy Gold caused controversy when she publicly disowned Destiny Etiko after Destiny hinted at having a new best friend.

Destiny flaunted her new best friend online, which angered Lizzy and led to her scolding Destiny.
