Aaron Cubrich Obituary-Death News; Member Of Operation 22 Til Freedom, Aaron Cubrich Passes Away

Member of Operation 22 Til Freedom Aaron Cubrich of Omaha, Nebraska has sadly passed away. He passed away leaving his family and friends devastated.

He was announced Dead on a post on facebook that reads” It is with saddened hearts, we regret to inform you that we have lost one of our very own. Aaron Cubrich unfortunately passed away this weekend.

Aaron served with Bravo Troop, 7-10 CAV, 4th Infantry Division from 2003-2007 out of Fort Hood.

Aaron was a newly appointed board member to Operation 22 til FREEDOM earlier this year, but has been a true believer and follower of OTTF since the beginning. His drive and valiant determination to help other Veterans will never be forgotten.

We ask everyone to pray for his family and loved ones in this difficult time. Also to respect their privacy. Operation 22 til FREEDOM be will helping set up a memorial service for Aaron and will post accordingly.”

Words fall short of expressing our grief for your loss, as we mourn with family and friends for this great loss. We are truly sorry to hear of the loss of this promising being.

Please accept our condolences, and may our prayers help comfort you. Please accept our heartfelt condolences.
