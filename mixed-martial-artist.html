Terry Etim is an English professional mixed martial artist with an estimated net worth of $150 thousand.

Terry Etim joined the Cage Gladiators Lightweight Championship where he defeated Greg Loughran via rear naked choke. He also fought Diego Gonzalez during the World Cage Fighting Championship in Manchester.

He trained under Pride veteran Luciano Azevedo in Brazil, in preparation for his debut at the Ultimate Fighting Championship. Etim faced several fighters during his stint with the UFC which include Matt Grice, Sam Stout, and Shannon Guherty. Eventually, he was released by the promotion, thus, prompting him to sign up with the Bellator, instead.

Terrence Lee Etim was born on January 11, 1986 in Liverpool, England.
