Jon Runyan Jr College, High School, Salary, Contract, NFL Draft, PFF, Jersey, Eagles Hall Of Fame

Jon Daniel Runyan Jr. is an American sportsman. He plays as the guard for the Green Bay Packers Team in the NFL.

Jon was born on the 8th of August, 1997 in Houston Texas. However, he is a native of Moorestown, New Jersey.

Runyan is the son of Congressman and former NFL player, Jon Runyan Sr., whom he was named after.

Jon Runyan Jr. College

Jon Runyan attended college in Michigan where he played football as an offensive tackle. Jon was named first-team All-Big Ten both in his junior and senior year of college.

Jon Runyan Jr. High School

Jon Runyan attended High school at St. Joseph’s Preparatory School in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Jon Runyan Jr. Salary

Jon Runyan is paid an average salary of $867,419 annually per his contract. However, in 2021, Runyan earns a base salary of $780,000.

Jon Runyan Jr. Contract

Jon Runyan signed a four-year contract with the Green Bay Packers worth $3,469,676. This includes a $174,676 signing bonus with $174,676 guaranteed.

Jon Runyan Jr. NFL Draft

Runyan was selected in the sixth round, 192nd overall pick of the 2020 NFL draft by the Green Bay Packers. He was signed on the 5th of June, 2020.

Jon Runyan Jr. PFF

Jon Runyan has a PFF grade of 65.8.

Jon Runyan Jr. Jersey

Jon Runyan’s Jersey is numbered 76 under the White, Gold and Dark Green colors of the Green Bay Packers.

Jon Runyan Sr. Eagles Hall of Fame

Jon Runyan Sr was inducted into the Eagles Hall of Fame this year.
