Do Remember! Rock The Bells Starts This Saturday In California

It’s baaaaaaack! For the seventh straight year, the Rock The Bells hip-hop festival will kick off this Saturday at the San Manuel Amphitheater in Devore, Calif. before taking the show on the road to the Shoreline Amphitheater in Mountain View, Calif. (August 27), Governors Island in New York City (September 3), and the Comcast Center in Mansfield, Mass. (September 10).

As usual, the festival will feature a diesel lineup. Among the projected highlights: Lauryn Hill performing The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill, Nas performing Illmatic, and Erykah Badu performing Baduizm, as well as performances by Cypress Hill, Black Star, Common, Raekwon and Ghostface, Mobb Deep, GZA, Immortal Technique, DOOM, Slaughterhouse, Mac Miller, Curren$y, and more. It’ll be hosted by The RZA, MURS, Supernatural, and Boy Jones.

If you don’t have tickets yet, get ’em now. And if you’re still not sure if the tickets are worth the price of admission, go ahead and read the paragraph above this one again. This is one show that you definitely do not want to miss this summer!
