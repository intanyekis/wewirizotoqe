Sophia Thomalla Bio (Wiki)

Who is Sophia Thomalla?

Sophia Thomalla is a German actress, presenter, and model.

She has appeared in movies and television series including ‘Neo Magazin’, ‘Schmidt – Chaos auf Rezept’ and ‘Mountain Medic’ among others.

Sophia Thomalla’s Early Life, Childhood, and Education

Thomalla was born in Berlin, Germany on October 6, 1989, to parents Simone Thomalla and André Vetters.

Simone is a famous actress. Throughout most of her childhood years, she grew up in Berlin until she moved to Cologne with her mother.

She ran Kickboxing as a recreational sport and played in some amateur fights early on in her career. She is of German nationality. Furthermore, she belongs to German ethnic background.

Talking about her education, Thomalla attended the Constantin School for Acting, Dance, and Singing in Bochum from 2007 to 2009.

Sophia Thomalla’s Career, Salary, and Net Worth

Thomalla initially made her screen debut in television with a small role in ‘Commissario Laurenti’ where she appeared as the daughter of Barbara Rudnik and Henry Hübchen.

In 2008, she portrayed the role of Helena Schön in ‘Our Charly’.

Additionally, in 2009, she appeared as Uschi Schuster in the TV series ‘Zeit der Entscheidung – Die Soap Deiner Wahl’. Since then, she has appeared in numerous other movies and television series. All in all, she has over 15 credits as an actress.

Some other movies and television series that Thomalla has appeared in are ‘Alarm für Cobra 11 – Die Autobahnpolizei’, ‘Einfach Magisch’, ‘Küstenwache’, ‘The Tricksters’, ‘The Berlin Project’, ‘The Last Cop’, ‘Countdown – Die Jagd beginnt’, ‘Eine wie Keine’, ‘Hanni & Nanni’,and ’SOKO Wismar’.

Additionally, she hosted the 10th SemperOperball in Dresden.

Thomalla was ranked third in the list of FHM Sexiest Women in 2010.  Furthermore, she applied for an open casting for ‘Germany’s Next Top Model’ in 2009.

Thomalla has not revealed her current salary. Additionally, there are no details available about her estimated net worth.
