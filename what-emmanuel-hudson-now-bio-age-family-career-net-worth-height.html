Emmanuel Hudson is Still Making Waves as an Entertainer

Emmanuel Hudson developed a passion for entertainment at a very young age and his brothers encouraged his talent. He could easily change the atmosphere with his funny gestures and jokes and was also a good rapper.

As such, he started making funny jokes with his brother Philip Hudson and in May 2009 the brothers decided to create a youtube channel “Emmanuel N Philip Hudson” where they would post short clips. Subsequently, they dabbled into singing and uploaded a hilarious song titled Ratchet Girl Anthem which made them famous.

This one shot into fame soon earned them a contract with Archive Entertainment and also a collaboration with the YouTube star Spoken Reasons on the song Why You Asking All Them Questions? which immediately went viral.

Later, Emmanuel made his TV debut on MTV’S Wild n Out. He was part of the show’s two seasons but left in the 7th season. In 2013 he made an appearance in another series titled Nashville and the following year, he had a cameo in the film Ride Along starring the likes of Ice Cube and Kelvin.

This also opened him to other roles in TV series such as Dead House, Blind Basketball, and the Netflix movie Step Sisters.

Asides from being an actor, Emmanuel would easily pass off as a writer, producer, and director. In this line, he has a couple of mini-series to boast of including the TV mini-series Down and Mind of EP.

The Ratchet Girl Anthem Crooner is 33 Years Old at the Moment

The YouTuber is the second youngest of 8 children born to his parents Roger and Brend Hudson on December 13, 1990. From what we garnered, he was raised in Mississippi, USA. However, his family later moved to Jonesboro, Georgia in 1998 when he was 8 years old.

While we can assert that Hudson is from a large family, not all his siblings are known. The only ones known to the public are Philip Hudson, Mattew Hudson, and Okeffe Hudson.

As for his education, he attended several high schools before enrolling at Fayette County High School where he graduated in 2009. After this, he proceeded to pursue his passion.
