Tyra Banks, Chrissy Teigen Go Makeup Free -- See Their &quot;Nude&quot; Look

Not afraid to go nude! Tyra Banks and Chrissy Teigen had nothing left to hide behind when they wiped every last bit of makeup off their faces during Monday, Sept. 28’s FABLife episode, “Nude Show.”

As you probably remember, Banks is no stranger to showing it like it is. The America’s Next Top Model host’s makeup-free selfie went viral back in June when she revealed the “real me” via Instagram, posing for a photo with zero makeup or filters.

But the 41-year-old model decided to take things a step further on Monday, when she inspired her fellow daytime talk show hosts to follow her lead, and brave a totally makeup free broadcast.

“Leah [Ashley], Lauren [Makk], Chrissy and I are going to be taking off our makeup right now, 100 percent, during the break,” an excited Banks revealed. “And when we come back, we’re going to be naked from the neck up,” she announced.
