Snoop Dogg Wiki / Biography

On October 20, 1971, he was born in Long Beach, California to parents Vernell Vernado and Beverly Tate. Vernell was born and named after his stepfather, Calvin Córdozer Broadus Sr., a Vietnam War soldier, musician, and mail carrier. His father was rarely present in his life. Broadus began singing and playing the piano as a child at Golgotha Trinity Baptist Church. He started rapping in sixth grade. Broadus supported himself as a child by selling candy, delivering newspapers, and getting meals.

Shante Taylor, Snoop's high school wife, married him on June 12, 1997. On May 21, 2004, Taylor filed for divorce, citing irreconcilable differences. The couple remarried on January 12, 2008. They have three children: two sons, Corday and Cordell, who gave up athletics to pursue careers as filmmakers, and a girl, Cory. Snoop Dogg Wiki- Early Life/Career

Early Life & Career: - Broadus began his recording career as Snoop Doggy Dogg. Dr. Dre initially collaborated with him on the theme song for the 1992 film Deep Cover, and later on Dr. Dre's debut solo album The Chronic with the other members of his previous beginning group, Tha Dogg Pound. This massive exposure helped Snoop Dogg's debut album, Doggystyle, achieve critical and commercial success.

Snoop Dogg performed alongside Dr. Dre, Eminem, Mary J. Blige, and Kendrick Lamar during the Super Bowl LVI Halftime Show. Snoop Dogg said in January 2022 that his 19th studio album, BODR, will be released on the same day as his participation at the Super Bowl Halftime Show. The album's release date, however, has been moved up two days to February 11, 2022. On February 10, 2022, Snoop Dogg announced his official acquisition of Death Row Records.
