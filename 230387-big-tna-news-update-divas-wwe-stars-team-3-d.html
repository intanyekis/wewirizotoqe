Big TNA News Update: Divas, WWE Stars, Team 3-D


Source: Pro Wrestling Torch Newsletter

TNA officially confirmed that Jushin “Thunder” Liger would be working the October 23rd Bound for Glory PPV event this past week. The company has yet to name an opponent for Liger at this time.

Gail Kim will be joining Traci Brooks as the only other regular female wrestler on TNA’s current roster. TNA may have already contacted several former WWE women’s wrestlers such as Jazz, Nidia and Jackie Gayda to help establish a new division.

As reported over the weekend, many former WWE stars were backstage at TNA’s Unbreakable PPV this past Sunday. Among those backstage were the former Dudley’s, Kevin “Mordecai” Fertig, Kenzo Suzuki & wife Hiroko, Andrew “Test” Martin and Shannon Moore.

Both Mark “Bubba Ray Dudley” Lamonica and Devon “D’Von Dudley” Hughes were backstage at the TNA Unbreakable PPV and were telling people that they have yet to sign a contract with the company yet, but expected to in time for the September 27th TV tapings for TNA’s debut on Spike TV. They were also overheard talking about why they felt Matt Hardy made a huge mistake in re-signing with WWE this year and adding that he would have “had it made” if he signed with TNA instead.

Jerry Lynn was backstage at the TNA PPV working in his usual role as an agent. Any future plans for his in-ring return have been derailed in TNA at the moment due to Sean Waltman no-showing the PPV and most likely not being welcome back.
