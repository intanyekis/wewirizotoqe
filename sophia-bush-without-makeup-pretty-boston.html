Sophia Bush Without Makeup Looks 10 Years Younger Bare-Faced Hollywood Life

Sophia Without Makeup — Flawless

The 30-year-old looked like a teenager wearing a teal t-shirt, black leggings and New Balance sneakers.

“So yesterday I auditioned for @CharlizeAfrica. And today she hired me for this…” she tweeted in late February. She included a link to a Deadline.com story about the show. Charlize Theron is the Executive Producer.

Sophia Bush Dishes On Her Makeup Must-Have

Thought it’s obvious she doesn’t need a stitch of makeup, Sophia told Us Weekly her beauty must-have is mascara!

“I have every brand under the sun.” And since mascara isn’t always enough, she’s also got a back-up stash of false eyelashes! “I’ve got a jar of them in my bathroom and I love it!”

Even though we usually see stars all glammed up on the red carpet, it’s nice to see them as nice, normal people on the street totally bare-faced!
