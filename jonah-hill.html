Jonah Hill, American Actor, Director

Jonah Hill is an American actor. His breakout role came in the 2007 comedy Superbad. Hill's most famous films include 21 Jump Street (2012), 21 Jump Street 2 (2014) and War Dogs (2016). He received his first Oscar nomination for portraying Peter Brand in 2011's Moneyball. Hill was nominated for another Oscar in 2014 following his performance in The Wolf of Wall Street alongside Leonardo DiCaprio. In 2018, Hill made his directorial debut with the drama Mid90s, which he also wrote.
