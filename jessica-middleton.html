Jessica Middleton's Mixed Martial Arts Career

Amateur Career

On 13th June 2015, Middleton made her amateur career debut at GFA – Organized Chaos 8, where she defeated her opponent Nici Kearney via TKO punches in round one. She next faced Julie Murray at TBC – Ultimate Blue Corner Battles. She lost the fight via unanimous decision.

At VFC 50 – Victory Fighting Championship 50, Middleton defeated Julie Murray in the round three via unanimous decision.

Professional Career

On 22nd July 2016, Middleton made her professional MMA career debut against Bruna Ellen at Bellator 159 – Caldwell vs. Taimanglo. She took the home belt in her first pro fight defeating her opponent via unanimous decision.

She next faced Alice Yauger at Bellator 171 – Guillard vs. Njokuani on 27th January 2017. She won the fight via unanimous decision.

In her third bout, Middleton faced Ilima-Lei Macfarlane at Bellator 178 – Straus vs. Pitbull 4. She lost the fight via armbar submission in the round one.

At Bellator 181 – Campos vs. Girtz, Middleton was defeated by Emily Ducote.

On 16th November 2017, he faced Denise Kielholtz at Bellator 188 – Lahat vs. Labiano. He lost the fight via submission due to scarf hold armlock.

On 18th May 2018, Middleton made his appearance at TFS – The Fight Series, where he faced Joselyne Edwards Laboriel. He lost the bout via TKO punches.
