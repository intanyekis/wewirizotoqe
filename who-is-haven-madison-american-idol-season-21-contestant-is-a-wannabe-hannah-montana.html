Who is Haven Madison? 'American Idol' Season 21 contestant is a 'wannabe Hannah Montana'

CLARKSVILLE, TENNESSEE: 'American Idol' returns to ABC on February 19 for the premiere of Season 21. In the upcoming season, there are many new performers who are just waiting to be discovered. 'Building 429' lead vocalist Jason Roy's daughter, Haven Madison, revealed on social media that she tried out for the reality competition. Her audition will be televised on the February 19 episode.

Haven started releasing pop music independently in 2020. At just 16, her mainstream-directed pop music is already making an impact on a growing fanbase of listeners, and she now has the opportunity to showcase her talent to a wider audience on 'American Idol'. Continue reading to find out more about Haven.

Who is Haven Madison?

Haven describes herself as a "wannabe Hannah Montana." Her Instagram bio reads, "Trying to survive high school while making music out of the creative ramblings of my restless mind." She’s a high school student from Clarksville, Tennessee, and has been a member of the cheerleading squad for some time. Haven grew up on a tour bus, traveling from state to state from the time she was two.

New cities, artistes and friends were discovered every day, and every night ended with a young Haven watching from the side of the stage while daydreaming of the moment when she would take the platform for herself. By the time she was eight, she had traveled to 46 of the lower 48 states and had started to compose music that reflected her own experiences.

Haven is a growing sensation in the music industry. She has released five singles, one collaboration and an EP to date. In 2020, she dropped her debut single, 'Already Gone,' 'Stronger', and followed that up. Since then, she has released a few more singles, including the aforementioned EP. Her latest song is called 'This Summer,' which you can catch on her YouTube channel. Haven has also garnered quite a presence on TikTok, with more than 4,500 followers. 

Internet in awe of about Haven Madison

Haven is among the singers who auditioned for Season 21 of 'American Idol'. She was one of three singers competing for America's platinum ticket, which was announced at the AMC Music Awards back in November 2022, and 'American Idol' has already released a clip of her audition. Though the fan-voted platinum ticket went to Elijah McCormick, Haven made quite an impression. The internet loved her performance and expressed its views. One user said, "Omg you’re so talented!!! Everyone at CHS is voting for you!" Another one added, "Wonderful job! Beautiful voice." Another user commented, "Very talented," while another added, "Outstanding! Plus, your guitar player ain't bad either!"

Haven won the golden ticket to Hollywood with her original song 'Fifteen'. Her father, Jason, was playing the guitar for her and could not hold back his tears when the judges gave her the golden ticket. 

 'American Idol' Season 21 debuts on February 19, at 8 pm ET/PT on ABC. The premiere episode will be available on Hulu.
