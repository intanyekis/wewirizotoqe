Kelly Rowland Has An &quot;R&amp;B Night&quot; At Jodeci, SWV, Dru Hill Concert

Fresh off of her Harlem’s Fashion Row honor as Fashion Icon Of The Year, Kelly Rowland flew back to the west coast to get her R&B life at the Summer Black Party Tour featuring headliners Jodeci and supporting acts SWV and Dru Hill.

Rowland shared footage from the festive evening on Instagram Friday (Sept. 8) of the Thursday night show at YouTube Theater at Hollywood Park in Inglewood, Calif. Within the clip, the Houston native and husband Tim Weatherspoon are seen singing along to Dru Hill’s “In My Bed”, Sisqo’s “Thong Song”, SWV’s “Right Here” and “I’m So Into You”, and Jodeci’s “Stay.”

The clip then switches to backstage footage of Rowland being greeted by SWV. All three members shared snaps of the loving encounter to their Instagram pages.

“Kaboom, [guess] who stepped in the room —Our baby @kellyrowland surprised us and came to the show to see us last night,” wrote LeLee Lyons. “Chile we were almost in tears. I couldn’t believe the stuff she remembered ll. Let’s just say I haven’t changed that much lol. Had a wonderful time
reminiscing. Oh what a time we had. This is why we do what we do—to create beautiful memories . So proud of all of my Destiny’s Child babies. Love you @kellyrowland.”

Kelly also penned a note to her “big sisters” via her Instagram Stories, writing, “So THIS HAPPENED LAST NIGHT, and I saw My Big Sisters, for the first time, in a long time! They killed their show, and I got to squeeze them Afterwards!!”

Rowland has been quite the jet-setter this week, as she attended all three of Destiny’s Child group mate Beyonce’s L.A. concerts before flying to New York to receive her New York Fashion Week award, only to immediately fly back to the Southern California city for the Summer Black Party Tour.

The cross-country R&B trek comes to a close tonight (Sept. 8) in Concord Calif.
