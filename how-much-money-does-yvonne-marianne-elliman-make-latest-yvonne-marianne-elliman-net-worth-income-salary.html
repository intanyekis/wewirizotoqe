Yvonne Marianne Elliman (born December 29, 1951) is an American vocalist who was a member of the original cast of the Broadway musical Jesus Christ Superstar for four years. In the 1970s, she had a string of hit singles, including “If I Can’t Have You,” which peaked at number one in the United States. After a lengthy break in the 1980s and 1990s, during which she devoted herself to her family, she returned to the music scene in 2004 with a solo album as a singer-songwriter.
