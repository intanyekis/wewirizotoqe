Danielle Fishel Maxim Cover: Girl Meets World Star Topanga Shows Off Major Cleavage in Sexy Lingerie

Boys, meet girl! Danielle Fishel has stripped down into lingerie for the super-sexy cover of Maxim‘s April issue that is sure to make jaws drop. Gearing up to reprise her role as Topango in the upcoming Boy Meets World spin-off, Girl Meets World, Fishel is giving fans a (big) glimpse of what they’ve been missing since the show ended in 2000.

The 31-year-old actress shows off major cleavage in a nude bra and flaunts her sexy, toned legs while posing seductively in a turquoise cardigan and pretending to chat on the phone.

Inside the issue — featuring more super-sexy shots — Fishel reveals how her famous Boy Meets World character Topanga got her name. “Michael Jacobs, who created Boy Meets World, says he was driving down the highway when production called and said, ‘We need a name for this character!’ He happened to be driving past Topanga Canyon, so he said, ‘Topanga,'” she reveals. “He says that if they had called him two miles later, I would’ve been named Canoga, which is the next exit.”

The actress, who recently became engaged to boyfriend Tim Belusko, also clears up reports that she dated her costar/onscreen love interest Ben Savage (aka Cory Matthews).
