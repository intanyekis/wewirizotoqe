Bella Hadid Salad Recipe Is The Perfect Everyday Meal

Eating healthy can be quite tricky, especially if anything uncooked forced a gag-like reflex from you. But this supermodel/salad lover has a recipe that is easy, super healthy, and surprisingly yummy for a healthy meal.

MORE: World’s Most Searched Recipe in 2022

Following in her big sister, Gigi Hadid, and best friend Hailey Bieber’s footsteps, Bella Hadid shared her favourite salad recipe on TikTok, which has surpassed more than two million views already. Her “Number one salad” recipe only consists of five main ingredients, making it perfect for anyone who’s not a fan of spending too much time in the kitchen.

Bella Hadid Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Arugula
  • Cucumbers
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Avocado
  • Lemon Juice
  • Balsamic Glaze
  • Olive Oil

Instructions:

Step 1: Take a big bowl and add fresh arugula with some finely chopped cucumbers, red peppers, and avocado. Top the veggies with crumbled parmesan cheese.

Step 2: For the dressing, add a few drops of lemon juice, balsamic glaze, and olive oil. You can also add salt and pepper according to taste.

Step 3: Add the dressing to the salad bowl and mix it all together.

Now, that’s the perfect meal to indulge in after a serious workout.
