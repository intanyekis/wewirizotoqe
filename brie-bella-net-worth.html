What is Brie Bella’s net worth and salary?

Brie Bella, the American professional wrestler, has a net worth of $12 million dollars, which she shares with her husband, Daniel Bryan, who is also a wrestler.

Brie and her twin sister, Nikki Bella, are known as the Bella Twins and have appeared on both the WWE and international circuits.

Brie Bella: Early Life and Career

Born in San Diego in 1983, Brie Bella grew up in Scottsdale, Arizona, where she excelled as an athlete in school. Together with her twin sister, Nicole, she pursued a career in modeling, promotions, and acting, leaving behind her initial focus on soccer. Brie graduated from Chaparral High School in 2002 and later attended college in San Diego before moving to Los Angeles to try and secure an agent. While looking for work in the entertainment industry, she supported herself by working as a server at the Mondrian Hotel.

Brie Bella’s Career in WWE

Brie Bella and her twin sister Nikki gained national attention on the Fox reality show “Meet my Folks” before becoming the Budweiser World Cup Twins. They were signed to developmental contracts by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) in 2007 and made their in-ring debut as the Bella Twins later that year. They began their main roster appearances in 2008 and developed an on-screen relationship with The Coltons. In 2009, they were both traded to the ECW brand but were later traded back to Raw. In 2011, they turned into villainous characters on Raw and focused on the Divas Champion. They returned to WWE in 2013 and began a feud with Natalya. They continued to appear at WWE live events and on “Twin Magic,” as well as competing in SummerSlams and on episodes of “Raw.” Brie announced her retirement on the March 10, 2019 episode of “Total Bellas” and was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2020 with her twin sister Nikki.

The Bella Twins’ Career Highlights

The Bella Twins have appeared on various television shows, including MTV’s “Ridiculousness”, and co-starred in a film called “Confessions of a Womanizer”. They have also appeared in music videos for Atreyu and Trey Songz. In 2014, they guest starred on the TV series “Psych” and presented an award at the MTV Europe Music Awards. They were nominated for Choice Female Athlete at the Teen Choice Awards in 2015 and 2016, winning the latter. The twins have a YouTube channel where they share fashion, beauty, travel, fitness, relationship, and health tips. They launched their own wine label, Bella Radici, in collaboration with Hill Family Estates and Gauge Branding in August 2017. They also launched their lifestyle intimates and activewear brand Birdiebee a few months later. In 2018, they launched a body and beauty line, Nicole + Brizee, and in March 2019, they started their own podcast. They have also appeared in eleven WWE video games.

Brie Bella’s Personal Life

Brie Bella began dating fellow wrestler Daniel Bryan in 2013, and they married in April 2014. They welcomed their first child in 2017 and announced they were expecting their second child on January 29th, 2020. Coincidentally, Brie’s twin sister announced her pregnancy the same day. Brie gave birth to a boy on August 1, 2020. Daniel and Brie are practicing vegetarians.
