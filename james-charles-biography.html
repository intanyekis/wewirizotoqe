James Charles Bio (Wiki)

Who is James Charles?

James Charles is an American Internet personality, model, and make-up artist.

He became the first male ambassador for CoverGirl. He announced his first Sisters Tour in April 2019.

James Charles: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

He was born on May 23, 1999, in Albany County, New York, USA.

He is 24 years old. His birth name is James Charles Dickinson and he is currently 23 years old.

His mother’s name is Christine Dickinson but his father’s name is unknown.

He has a brother named Ian Charles. He is openly gay and his parents support him and motivate him to be better.

James Charles holds American citizenship and his ethnicity is Caucasian.

Education

He graduated from Bethlehem Central High School in 2017.

During his graduation, he wore CoverGirl’s slogan “Easy, Breezy, Beautiful”.

James Charles: Professional Life, Career

He launched his YouTube channel on December 1, 2015, and uploaded videos focusing on makeup.

His channel has 13.4 million subscribers and over 1.7 billion views on YouTube.

On May 11, 2019, he became the first-ever YouTube personality to lose 1 million subscribers in 24 hours.

He has lost 3 million subscribers as of May 14, 2019, because of the public controversy with Internet personality Tati Westbrook.

James Charles became the first male spokesmodel for CoverGirl on October 11, 2016, working alongside brand ambassador Katy Perry.

Later in January 2019, he was invited to Bringham, England, to open the company’s second UK store.

James Charles did the makeup for Iggy Azalea’s music video shoot for ‘Sally Walker in March 2019 and also he made a cameo appearance in the video.

Awards, Nominations

James Charles won the award for the best channel in the Beauty category at the 8th Streamy Awards.

James Charles: Net Worth, Salary

He has an estimated net worth of around $12 million and he has earned that sum of money from his professional career.

Also, he gets around $5 million (yearly) as a salary.

James Charles: Rumors, Controversy/Scandal

He became involved in controversy after he tweeted an offensive joke about Africa and Ebola in February 2017 but he later apologized via a statement.

In March 2017, YouTube makeup artist Thomas Halbert posted screenshots of previous conversations with Charles.

James Charles admitted his story regarding his rise in notability was falsified and he originally claimed he had his high school prom photographs retaken with a ring light and it is revealed that he had actually edited the photographs.

Tati Westbrook uploaded a video titled ‘BYE SISTER…’ and accused Charles of ‘manipulating people’s sexuality, and ‘using…fame, power, and money to play with people’s emotions.

Charles’ subscribers dropped by over one million in less than 24 hours.

Later, he uploaded a response video and apologized to his fans and both her and her husband.

Body Measurements: Height, Weight

He has a height of 5 ft. 9 inches and weighs 63 kg.

James Charles has brown eyes and black hair.

Other information regarding James’ body measurement, shoe size, and dress sizes, is not available.
