Currently playing as the power forward for the Miami Heat of the National Basketball Association, Chris Bosh officially joined the NBA when he was selected as the fourth overall pick by the Toronto Raptors in the first round of the 2003 NBA Draft. Chris Bosh actually belonged to a strong draft class which include future NBA sensations such as Dwayne Wade, Carmelo Anthony and LeBron James. As of 2015, Chris Bosh has an estimated net worth of $110 million.

In July 2010, he officially signed a deal with the Miami Heat which teamed his up with Dwayne Wade and LeBron James. In a game against the Atlanta Hawks in January 2012, Chris Bosh lead the Miami Heat to a 116–109 triple overtime win with the absence of the injured Wade and James. He finished the game with 2 blocked shots, 2 steals, 5 assists, 14 rebounds and 33 points.
