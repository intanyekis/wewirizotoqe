Scottish chef, television personality and restaurateur, Gordon Ramsay has an estimated net worth of $175 million in 2015.

The short-tempered chef Gordon Ramsay is rarely off our television screens - or out of the newspapers.

This is thanks to his larger than life personality, loud mouth and extra-marital shenanigans as well as his top-notch culinary skills and ambition for worldwide domination of food-themed programming and the posh restaurant market from here to Australia.

This magic list of ingredients, including a ratings hit in the US with Ramsay's Kitchen Nightmares and Hell's Kitchen and a string of Michelin-starred restaurants including his eponymous three star rated London eaterie which boasts a turnover of £80,000 a month.

But Ramsay's trademark bad temper, a tendency to overstretch the brand and a large dash of the credit crunch has spoilt the broth in the last few years plunging his company Gordon Ramsay Holdings into serious debt and shrinking his own cash mound to nearer £40m.

According to the Daily Mail, his bank RBS, which allowed his company a £10.6m overdraft in 2006, last year secured a 'unilateral notice' against Ramsay's £3m seven-bedroom home by Wandsworth Common, South-West London as security against the company's debts.
