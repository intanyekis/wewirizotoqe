Who Is Joe Burrow? Joe Burrow Wiki

Joe Burrow Wiki: - Joe Burrow was born on December 10, 1996, in Ames, Iowa, U.S. He is an American football quarterback for the Cincinnati Bengals of the National Football League (NFL). Burrow played collegiate football at LSU after attending Ohio State, where as a senior he won the 2020 collegiate Football Playoff National Championship and the Heisman Trophy. In the 2020 NFL Draft, the Bengals picked him first overall.

Following a rookie season cut short by injuries, Burrow bounced back in his second season, guiding the Bengals to their first postseason victory since 1990, snapping the longest current losing streak among the four major North American sports, and making an appearance in Super Bowl LVI.

Burrow has guided the Bengals to five postseason victories in his three seasons as their quarterback the same number of victories the team had previously achieved before choosing him. By sportswriters and other players, Burrow has been dubbed "Joe Cool" and "Joe Brrr" for his calmness under pressure.
