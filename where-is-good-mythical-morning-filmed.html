Where Is Good Mythical Morning Filmed?

Good Mythical Morning is filmed at Mythical Entertainment in Burbank, California. The studio is founded by Rhett and Link.

Mythical is an Internet-first entertainment studio founded by two friends. They have eight channels under this company.

The company’s headquarters lies in Burbank, California. They shoot all of their shows and podcasts in the same building.

Similarly, the team pre-films videos a week or even a month before they are scheduled to be uploaded according to a production schedule.

After completing the shooting for the week or months, they plan for more shows with the show’s writer.

Each show has its own setup in the building. For Good Morning Mythical, they have stand-up desks where they film the entire episode.

Rhett and Link segregate their work and make it simple to achieve the desired result for the particular shows.

As the hosts belong to engineering backgrounds, they believe in having systems in place which maximize the output.

Every crew member have maintained the system in the locations of shooting, and along with that, they have enough break time.

Their everyday routine is planning for the content with their other associates and finalizing the shoot for the day in advance.

The team member has a brief description of the content needed to make and upload for the audience and the channel.

Having a clear idea about the trending topic helps them to prepare to sustain among their audiences and make quality videos.

The show’s fans also engaged in an interesting debate about the shooting location and episodes of the show on Reddit.

Fans believed that the show’s creators were attempting to keep the filming location secret from their target audience.

However, the speculations have been cleared by some informed viewers who were aware of the show’s filming locations.
